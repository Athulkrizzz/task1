KERALA BLASTERS

Kerala Blasters FC, often referred to as KBFC, was founded in 2014 with the vision of rekindling the footballing spirit in Kerala, a state that has produced some on the finest footballers in India. The aim was not only to compete but also cultivate the footballing ecosystem in the state.

HOME STADIUM

Our Home, Jawaharlal Nehru International Stadium, popularly known as the Kaloor Stadium situated in Kochi, Kerala is one of India’s largest and most modern venues. Completed in 1996, ahead of schedule, by the Greater Cochin Development Authority (GCDA), the stadium has a unique shape owing to the triple tiered stands, sloping and contemporary roof structure, modern turf, four-lane road from the southern side of the stadium and massive floodlight masts going through the roof. The lighting towers of 2kW flood lights is one of the most innovative aspects of the stadium, which when switched on fully can provide lighting levels for an HD telecast.
